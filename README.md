# Job Search Playbook

Democratizing AI for college graduates — a complete job search guide using Claude AI and the Harvard Career Services framework to level the playing field.

## What This Is

A free, comprehensive playbook that teaches college students and early-career professionals how to use AI tools (Claude, Perplexity) to compete at the highest level in their job search — regardless of background, network, or resources.

First-generation college students have significantly smaller professional networks. AI tools democratize access to expert-level career guidance that was previously only available through expensive career coaches, strong family networks, or elite university connections.

## What's Inside

### Phase 0: Pre-Launch
- LinkedIn profile optimization with AI prompts
- Social media audit and online presence cleanup
- Portfolio and GitHub setup

### Phase 1: Foundation & Setup
- Building your AI command center (Claude Projects)
- Custom instructions for career-focused AI assistance
- Document upload strategy (resume, transcripts, samples)

### Phase 2: Application Materials
- Resume optimization with keyword analysis and ATS checks
- Cover letter generation using Harvard's three-paragraph structure
- Application tracking and the 70/20/10 rule
- Quality tiers: dream jobs vs. good fits vs. volume plays

### Phase 3: Intelligence Gathering
- Company research and SWOT analysis prompts
- LinkedIn networking and decision-maker outreach
- Connection message templates (under 300 characters)

### Phase 4: Interview Preparation
- Interview question prediction (behavioral, technical, situational)
- Mock interview roleplay system with dual personas
- Video interview technical setup checklist
- STAR story bank creation

### Phase 5: Execution & Follow-Up
- Strategic thank-you email system (only 24% of candidates send one)
- Follow-up timeline without being annoying
- Reference request templates and preparation packages

### Phase 6: The Finish Line
- Job offer evaluation framework with 2025 salary data
- Salary negotiation strategy (18.8% average increase for those who try)
- Acceptance email template

## Key Stats

- Only **24%** of candidates send thank-you emails, yet **68%** of hiring managers say it affects decisions
- Candidates who negotiate salary see an average **18.8% increase**, yet **55%** never try
- **95%** of recruiters use LinkedIn to find candidates
- Average entry-level job search: **200-400 applications**
